~ ~ ~ Welcome to Legally Distinct Greatest Idea Mafia! ~ ~ ~

Hosted by @wlwillow and Somebody hopefully

Phases

Pregame: 24 Hours Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Starting Phase: Day 1. There is a Day 1 Execution. Action Lock: Night actions lock an hour before the end of the night. Day actions lock an hour before the end of the day.

Voting

Majority: Enabled D2+ Plurality: Enabled No Execution: Enabled Abstaining: Random Elimination among all living players. Ties: Random Elimination among the tied players. No Execution will always win ties.

Game Mechanics

Cause of Death: Off. There is no kill credit. Flips: Full unless otherwise stated. Feedback: Limited. Investigatives will learn if their action fails or if is redirected. No other feedback unless otherwise stated. Last Wills: Off. There are no last wills. Actions: May only target alive players (not self) unless otherwise stated. May not multitask unless otherwise stated. Alignment: See Setup. Scum factions are the Mafia, the Werewolves, the Cult, the Aliens, and any Serial Killers. Judas and Saulus-aligned players count as scum for rolling purposes, and a non-town 1-shot Recruiter counts as 2 scum. Win Conditions: The Town wins by killing all members of the scum factions. The scum factions (except Serial Killers) win by eliminating all other scum factions, and then reaching parity with the Town. Neutral win conditions are specified on their rolecards. Factional Kills: The Mafia, the Werewolves, and any Serial Killers all have their own factional kill. The Aliens have a factional bloodsucker. The Cult has a 1-shot factional conversion that refunds upon failure.

Activity

- 15 messages are required every 24 hours. Failing to meet this requirement will result in a prod. - No posting for 24 hours results in 2 prods. - 3 prods and you will be forcefully replaced.
2:49 p.m.

Setup - Up to 25 Players

Every player receives three cards from the "deck" (as listed in a thread). Each player then chooses one of the cards to be their alignment, one to be their ability/role, and one to discard. You may not choose any more than one card for each of these. This is the pregame phase. If you do not choose by the deadline, it will be randomized. Then, the host will check the setup to ensure that at least 60% of the game is Town-aligned, with caveats for alignment changers. If there is not, random players will be forcibly converted to Town until the threshold is met. The host also reserves the right to reroll the game anyways, starting another pregame phase. Then, scumchats are created, masons/lovers are paired, and so on, and then Day 1 begins. The "deck" does not deplete at any point, it's just called the deck.

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible. Additionally, the setup has the following extra rules:
  • You may not discuss the pregame phase. Doing so will be considered angleshooting. This includes discussing what a player would pick for their alignment or role, or discussing or claiming what cards you recieved. Do not claim what cards you received, or if you were forcibly converted!

Balance is not guranteed in this setup.
