Welcome to CO11 - Caitlin's Invitational!

This will be a special event game run by @Caitlin, where the players have been added via an invitational phase. YOU can sign up as a spectator, in order to watch these players play, and win prizes through minigames that will happen throughout the game! How the game will work is described below, after that, we will go over the minigames and spectator events!

Phases

Starting Phase: Daystart Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Day Starting Time: At 22 UTC0 -> 22 UTC 0 = 6:00 p.m. in your timezone

Setup

11 Town vs 3 Mafia | 11v3 | 14 Players Total + 1x 1-Shot Day-2 Vigilante, if attacked N1 you will survive, but lose your shot. + 10x Citizen (Vanilla Townie) - 3x Goon (Vanilla Mafia)

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled - If a player is voted by half of the currently living players +1 then the day immediately ends with that players death. Ties: Resolved by RNG - If two or more players are tied in votes when the day ends, a random one is chosen to be eliminated. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is eliminated. Lylo: Announced + Votelock - Lylo will be announced, once this happens, votes will be locked in once placed and may not be changed for the remaining days.

Mafia Mechanics

Mafia have a 24/7 private chat. The Mafia has a factional kill with which they can kill a player each night, usage of this kill is mandatory. - If no player is chosen as the target, a random player will be killed by the mafia each night.

Activity Requirements

Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 50 times each 24 hours of a day phase. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 50 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example.

Spectator Events

If you sign up for this game, you will sign up as a Spectator. This will make you eligible for joinin in on spectator activities. We will be holding two different events during this game, both of which can reward you with prizes! Event 1: Guess Your Hero!
  • Choose a player during day 1, if that player survives and wins the game then both you and that player win 1 month of Nitro Basic.
  • If nobody guesses correctly, the people who guessed a player on the winning team, who survived the longest, win.
  • If multiple people win, the prize is given to a random one of them.
Event 2: Night 1 Mafia Guessing!
  • Choose three players during night 1, if you are correct, you win 1 month of Nitro or 3 months of Nitro Basic.
  • If nobody guesses all three Mafia correctly, then the next best guess wins.
  • If multiple people win, a random one wins the prize!
