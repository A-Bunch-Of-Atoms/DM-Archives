-- 2D3 - NE30 (9 Players) -- Hosted by Giles Corey and Lunexia Introduction: Welcome all to NE30! The setup for this Newcomer game is 2D3, a popular beginner's setup across forum mafia. In this setup, there are 9 possible options for power roles, based on two D3s being rolled. Rules: Most of these rules are unsaid or instead discouraged, but since this is a newcomers game I'll give some rules for this game here. 1. You are expected to follow every Game Rule found in #rules. 2. Always play for your own faction's win. 3. Do not attempt to encrypt messages in order to communicate with another player. 4. Do not use any form of provable randomness. This includes using Discord bots where any other player can see it, screenshots of RNG websites, etc. Saying you used RNG is fine, as long as there isn't any proof that you did so. 5. Do not copy and paste or screenshot anything from your player chat. Paraphrasing is fine. 6. Stupid Rule: If anything I feel you are doing is an attempt to circumvent or create a loophole for any of the rules, I will take what I feel is appropriate action. 7. Have fun! This is meant to be a casual game and a learning experience. General Mechanics: Day phases will be 48h, Night phases will be 24h. A private mafia chat will be open at all times during the game. You must send 25 messages every 24 hours during the day phase or you will be prodded. 3 prods will result in a replacement. If a day ends due to a majority vote before 24 hours have passed, there will be no prods. Majority will be enabled, meaning if over half the players vote a player, the day will end immediately. Plurality will also be enabled, meaning if 48 hours have passed during the day with no one having received a majority of the votes, the player with the most votes will be lynched and the day will end. If half the players (rounded up if odd amount) vote "No Lynch", then nobody will be lynched that day, and the day will end immediately. If a vote is tied, it will lead to a no vote. Upon a player being lynched, their role and which guest they were will be revealed. Here is the Discord Mafia page that shows how the setup operates, along with all the possible roles and what they do: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/2d3 Feel free to ask Lune or me any questions you have!
