~ ~ ~ Welcome to MA70: Pick Your Poison! ~ ~ ~

Welcome to Pick Your Poison (Poison, not Power )! This is a 13P setup with 7 citizens, 3 town power roles, and 3 goons. However, in this game, before it starts, every town will only be told their alignment, while the mafia will have 24 hours pregame to pick the 3 town power roles... Yes, the mafia picks the PR's in a pool of 5 possible ones! These will be rolled randomly between the 10 town players, allowing for them to know their final role before game starts.

Setup

+ 7 Citizens + 0-1 Innocent Child + 0-1 Tracker + 0-1 1-Shot Cop + 0-1 Jailkeeper + 0-1 2-Shot Vigilante - 3 Goons

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Always Enabled Majority: Enabled D2+, Enabled D1 after 24 hours. NoLynch: Enabled D2+ Ties: First to reach. No Votes: No Lynch

Game Mechanics

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Mafia has a 24/7 private chat. The Innocent Child here would choose when to be revealed by the host (instead of being revealed automatically at the start of the game).

Activity Requirements

Prods: At least 25 posts each 24 hours. - These 25 posts must be in relation to the game. - 0 posts in 24h will result in 2 prods. - If you get 3 prods, you will be replaced. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1h+ each day).

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.

Additional Servers

Please join the following server, it is required for this game.
