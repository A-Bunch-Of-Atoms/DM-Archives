It also has many alternate universes for skins, for those who are interested in that. What about Legends of Runeterra, Teamfight Tactics, and Wild Rift? Since they take place in the same universe as League of Legends, you can also choose something from those. The lore is expansive, and so are you choices. Where can I find info about League of Legends? There is quite a bit of info about it on the official site, https://www.leagueoflegends.com, and the fan made wiki, https://leagueoflegends.fandom.com/wiki/League_of_Legends_Wiki.
