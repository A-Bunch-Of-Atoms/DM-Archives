Game Mechanics

Phases - The game starts with a day phase (Day One). - Day Phases: 48 hours. - Night Phases: 24 hours. Voting - Majority: Enabled - If a player is voted by half the living players rounded up, they immediately die, ending the day. - Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 will die, granted majority was not reached. - Ties: Ties are resolved by rng between the tied players. - Free voting: You may change your vote however many times you wish during regular days. - Lylo votelock: During lylo situations (2v3 or 1v2), votes will be locked, meaning you only get 1 vote that day. Mafia/Mason - Private chats are 24/7. - The Mafia's faction kill is enforced, not choosing a target will result in an RNG kill. - The Mafia Night 1 Rolecop may multitask should the Goon die day 1. Activity - A mimimum of 30 game related (non-fluff/offtopic/spam) posts is expected every 24h. - Failure to meet this requirement will result in a prod/warning. - 3 warnings will result in an exclusion from the game and a forced replacement.
