~ ~ ~ Welcome to Jackal Returns ~ ~ ~

Hosted by @WillowThePillow and @Danger?! Icet!

Phases

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Starting Phase: Day 1. There is a Day 1 Execution. Action Lock: Night actions lock an hour before the end of the night. Day actions lock 2 hours before the end of the day.

Voting

Majority: Enabled D2+ Plurality: Enabled No Execution: Enabled Abstaining: Random Elimination among all living players. Ties: Random Elimination among the tied players. No Execution will always win ties.

Game Mechanics

Cause of Death: Off. There is no kill credit. Flips: Full unless otherwise stated. Feedback: Limited. Investigatives will learn if their action fails or if is redirected. No other feedback unless otherwise stated. Last Wills: Players may leave behind a 200 character max message to be revealed on their death. Actions: May only target alive players (not self) unless otherwise stated. May not multitask. Coven Actions: Coven members may submit private actions in their player chat which override any actions stated in Coven chat. Coven members only receive their own feedback. Necronomicon: Each day, the Coven votes on which Coven member receives the Necronomicon at the beginning of the night. If there is no chosen member, it goes to the Coven Head, then a random Coven member. In the case of a 1v1 between a Coven member and Jackal Recruit, the Coven member will always win ties.

Activity

- 15 messages are required every 24 hours. Failing to meet this requirement will result in a prod. - No posting for 24 hours results in 2 prods. - 3 prods and you will be forcefully replaced. (edited)
