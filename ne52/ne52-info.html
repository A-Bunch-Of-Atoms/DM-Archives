Setup:

  • 5 Town Citizens
  • 1 Mafia Goon
  • The remaining 3 roles will be chosen by picking randomly a number in 1/2/3 and a letter in A/B/C that will determine the row and column that contain the roles that will be played. Need more info on the setup? Click here! https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/2d3 ### Game Mechanics:
Day Phase: 48 hours long Night Phase: 24 hours Mafia Chat: Available 24/7 Factional Kill: Mandatory. If someone is not chosen, a random townie will be killed.

Voting Mechanics:

Majority: Enabled on D2 Plurality: Enabled No-Lynch: Enabled on D2 Flips: Full Alignment/Role Tied Votes: First to Reach No Votes: Random Lynch

Activity Requirements:

20 game-related posts are required each 24 hours of a day phase. If you do not reach this goal, you will be prodded. If you receive three prods, you will be forcefully replaced. Zero posts will result in a double prod. Exceptions to prods/replacements can be made at the mod’s discretion.

Global Rules

All rules not mentioned above that are in #📜rules also apply!
