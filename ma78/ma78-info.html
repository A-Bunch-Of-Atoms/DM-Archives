GAMBLING ADDICTION Hosted by @Lagavulin is sometimes a tree 🍃 and @Ngekop Gambling Addiction is a renaming of D6, a 13 player game from Mafia Universe. It's a semi-open setup but rather than a simple matrix of possible roles, six six-sided dice are rolled for an interesting twist for those of us who like slightly more exciting probablities.

Setup

10 Town 3 Mafia Six 6-sided dice will be rolled. Each 1-3 triggers investigative, each 4-5 triggers protective, and each 6 triggers killing. The amount of rolls for each category of role decides the role that will be in play. Investigative 1: Innocent Child 2: Voyeur 3: Motion Detector 4: Role Cop 5: Tracker 6: Parity Cop Protective 1: Bodyguard 2: Doctor 3: Roleblocker 4: Jailkeeper 5: Jailkeeper + Bodyguard 6: Roleblocker + Doctor Killing 1: 1-shot Vigilante 2: 2-shot Vigilante 3: Vigilante 4: Vigilante 5: Vigilante + 1-shot Vigilante 6: Vigilante + 2-shot Vigilante Mafia Based on the amount of town PRs, mafia will also get some PRs. If town has 1, mafia gets 0. If town gets 2, mafia gets 1. If town has 3, mafia gets 2. One die is rolled for each mafia role. Depending on the roll they get: 1: Mafia Roleblocker 2: Mafia Roleblocker 3: Mafia Jailkeeper 4: Mafia Role Cop 5: Mafia Tracker 6: Mafia Watcher The full setup inclulding rolling mechanic, role cards, and an example roll is here: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1siIaNNX1qCot9Ov55oNMpMb770cy2yD8oljXuNIpd1s/edit?usp=sharing *Credit to Caitlin for writing the linked setup document. I just consolidated, rearranged, and renamed it.

Phase Mechanics

Days: 48h Nights: 24h

Vote Mechanics

Plurality: Always Enabled Majority: Enabled after d1 Skipping: Enabled after d1 Ties: RNG eliminaton among tied Abstaining: RNG elimination among all living players

Factional Mafia Mechanics

Multitasking: On Daytalk: On

Feedback and Game Mechanics

Flips: Roles flip upon all deaths Kill Credit: None Night Feedback: None, except for investigative abilities

Activity Requirements

Minimum: 25 posts / 24h, each time you miss you will receive a prod Warnings: 3, after 3 prods you will be force replaced Host discretion may alter these rules in special cases Please only join if you have time to play!

Global Rules

All rules not mentioned above that are in #📜rules also apply!
