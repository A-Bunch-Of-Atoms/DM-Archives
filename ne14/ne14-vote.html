Vote Explanation @Alive Line NE This will explain how voting works and how you vote other players. With 9 players alive at the start of the game, a player needs to be voted by 5 players for a hammer to occur. Majority voting: Enabled Plurality voting: Enabled No Lynch: Disabled day 1, enabled on any day after that If you need to look up any of the above terms, consult this helpful wiki article: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/Voting_Terminology To vote another player type /vote [Name]. To unvote type /unvote You can change your vote to another player by typing /vote [Name] again, you do not have to unvote to do that. Voting is done in this channel. Please only use this channel for voting and keep conversations in #ne14-chat There will be regular "vote counters" which I will post for you where you can check up on who is currently voting! Voting is one of the town's strongest tools, as it is the only way to rid yourselves from the Mafia, use it, and use it a lot. Not using it means no Mafia member can die. (edited)
