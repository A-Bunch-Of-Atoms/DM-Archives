Ghost Realm

(I'm bad at naming things) ###

So this is an experimental game, so may go a bit wacky. Anyway, during this game, all living players are just Citizens and Goons, but once they die, they gain abilities that they can use at night to help out their team. (Carry your team while dead.) Dead players will have access to a dead chat. When a player dies, they will be able to choose a role from the list of roles. Each role is unique. If they do not choose, they will be assigned a special role. Phases: Day: 48 hours Night: 24 hours Voting Mechanics: Plurality: Always enabled. - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled after Day 1. - If a player is voted by half of the currently living players +1 then the day immediately ends with that player's death. NoLynch: Disabled. - Voting for no lynch is not an option. Ties: Resolved by first - If two or more players are tied in votes when the day ends, the first one to reach the tie is chosen to be lynched. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is lynched. Mafia Mechanics: Mafia has a private 24/7 chat. Mafia cannot decide to no kill. Flip Mechanics: Living players, upon dying, will flip the role they assume. Dead players, upon dying again, will flip their alignment. Night kills will be announced with the role/faction of the killer. Activity Requirements: Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 20 times each 24 hours, otherwise you'll receive a prod. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. - If you don't post once in a 24h period, you'll receive 2 prods instead. - If you receive 3 prods, you'll be force-replaced. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 20 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it that would see you hitting these requirements with ease. Rules: Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our ⁠#rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them. Breaking our rules can lead to disciplinary action, such as game bans, or forceful replacement by a different player- which we would like to avoid if at all possible.
