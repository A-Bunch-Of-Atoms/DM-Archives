Post-colonial Pandemonium (for 21 players)

Hosted by @kirigiri and @Lagavulin - with balancing provided by @Danger!? Luka! In a certain recently-minted democracy in Western Africa, the penumbra of chaos looms overhead. Our dear citizens' hard won peace is once again under threat; the Americans have decided that the populace have been taking their right to self-determination a little too seriously, and have dispatched two of their crack agents onto the scene to try to stage a coup and subvert the will of the people! Add to the mix all manner of chancers and renegades relishing to exploit the situation, and the our country has a full blown crisis on its hands! *Will the citizens prevail and protect their state from foreign meddling? Or will the country descend into Post-colonial Pandemonium?! *

Overview

In this game, every player will have some actions they can take, and (almost) every action requires spending **money**. Different characters receive money in different ways, though all **Citizens** will receive $1 million per night. Money may be also transferred from player to player at any time if it is agreed. Every player will also receive a **Character Name** as flavour; some of these are of course pre-determined, however **Citizen's names will be randomly rolled from a list of given names and surnames below, feel free to use one yourself if you are in need of a 'cover identity'! There are several factions in this game: each with their own win condition, some of which may be compatible with others! Anti-citizen factions will have a set list of possible characters that can be generated; not every role listed will end up in the final game! All players may, per each Day phase, send a maximum of 3 private messages to any other living player in their own player-chat. These messages may also be used to transfer funds or items, or to offer trades. You should ping hosts to ensure they are delivered promptly! ( link to name list: https://pastebin.com/hrLhtv76 )

Factions

CIA (2 players) Factional Actions: - CIA faction begins the game with $10m, and share a factional chat. - ($5m) If the President is dead, may attempt to install a dictator by nominating a player; may not coincide with any other night actions. Player must be eligible to become dictator and must survive the night in order for the CIA to win. - ($1m per attack) May attack any living player at night, may do this as many times as they are eligible (default is 1 but may be as many as 3). CIA attacks are never revealed to their target. - ($0) May send up to three anonymous messages per night to any players, who may respond. Win Condition: Will win if any members of the Separatists or Military are installed as Dictator, or if either are alive at the end of the game. Possible Characters: Hugo Rider [the Interceptor] - Each night, selects one player to bug - Will receive all incoming and outgoing private communications from bugged players, in the next Day phase - Bugged players will not automatically know they are bugged Dallas Frankincense [the Embezzler] - Each night, chooses one player and siphons the funds they earn at night - If the same player is visited twice, they learn the identity of the Embezzler Ariadne Black [The Saboteur] - Each night, chooses one player and blocks them from completing their action; actions that require money to complete still cause the target to lose their money Samantha Sharp [the Assassin] - Once per game, in addition to the factional kill, may attack one player during the Night that penetrates bulletproof vests; if the player targeted resists the attack; the Assassin dies. May not use this power on Night 1. Cecil Darvish [the Veteran] - Learns the identity and role of any player that visits them. Separatists (2 players) Factional Actions: - Do not share a factional kitty or chat by default. Win Condition: Will win if either are installed as dictator, or if they are alive when all four members of the Civilian government and both Military Leaders are dead. If one Separatist is installed as dictator then even if the other is alive, the other does not win the game. Possible Characters: Dennis Sabaly [the Arms Dealer] - Each night produces two guns if not visited, which may be kept or distributed; may not use these guns themselves Roland Malgré [the Armour Dealer] - Each night produces two bullet-proof vests if not visited, which may be kept or distributed; may not use these vests themselves Rigobert Onana [the Oil Baron] - Each night earns $3m - Each night may target as many players as is possible for them, and if they do, spend $1.5m per player to learn their factional allegiances.
