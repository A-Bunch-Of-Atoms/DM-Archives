Alright, let's dive into the various non-standard settings, let's start with the voting/day phase system. Day Mechanics (Voting) Nomination Voting. The first 24 hours of the day will have a nomination section. Alive players can nominate one other player. If there were any nominations, the day is extended by another 24 hours, these can be used to vote the nominated players. Living players may vote one nominated player of their choice. During this time everyone may continue to discuss normally. This game will feature whispers, here's how they will work. Regular Whispering Each player can send 3 whispers of 2000 characters or less per day. The recipient may respond without expending a whisper of their own. These whispers are publicly announced if cast, both the sender and recipient will be known. Demon Whisper Once per game the demon may whisper another player with a message of 2000 characters or less. This whisper is not announced. You may target a dead player with this whisper. Players will not be eliminated from the game should they die. Death Mechanics Upon death, players may continue to talk normally. All their roles abilities and attributes become disabled however. Unless specified they can no longer be targeted by abilities either. They may no longer nominate or whisper or receive whispers. They get one "Vote Token". They may use their token to vote on a nominated player once, expending the token. If a dead player has no vote token, then they may no longer vote. The demon may kill itself, this is called Star Pass If the Demon kills itself at night, then a random minion becomes the demon's role at the start of the following day. The demon will know roles not in the game to fake claim. Bluffs The demon will learn 3 Town/Outcast roles that are not in the game.
