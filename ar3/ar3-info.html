Settlers of Catan Expanded

A standard game of Settlers of Catan has a maximum of 4 players excluding the host. The rules to the base game of Catan can be found here. The game will be resolved through Tabletop Simulator (I'll take screenshots of the boardstate each turn)(If you have TTS, then you will be able to view the board at anytime you wish) but actions will be submitted and processed through me in your player chats or in the public channel. Because I would like more people to play, depending on how many people sign up, I will add expansions to the base game to give more space for builds and victory points. This game may take a while, and while not the most socially heavy game, it is highly likely that you will need to negotiate with others.

Gameplay

Each player has a maximum of 24 hours per turn to do as they wish. A player may end their turn at any time before that if they have no more actions they wish to do. At the start of a turn, a number will be publicly rolled by me or a scheduled bot, and all players will publicly receive their resources directly after. The player whose turn it is can build/place/move pieces by sending me a message in their player chat. This player can also trade with players by reaching verbal agreements in the public chat (both players must publicly confirm their agreement). Only the player whose turn it is can trade with other players, but players can try to initiate trades with them.

Other Rulings

  • Please do not try to strategize or talk about the game outside of the player chats or public chat channels. I may open private chats if required by expansions, but otherwise only talk about the game with others in the public chat channel
  • If you would like to not do any actions, please inform me. Failure to submit any actions or a declaration of idling will result in a prod.
  • Two prods in a row, or a high amount of prods throughout the game will result in you being replaced out.
    • If a replacement is unable to be found in a timely manner, I will modkill the slot and, depending on how far through the game it is, may leave the ruins of the player's builds on the field to play around.
    ## Signups
  • When you sign up, please DM me a preference list of colors you would like to be.
  • This game may last a while, please make sure you can commit to it.
    • If you would like to play with another player as a hydra, please send me a message in the signups channel.
