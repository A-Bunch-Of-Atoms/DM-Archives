Welcome to NE29, a NewD3 newcomer game! Here in #ne29-info , Lune and I will give relevant information and announcements for this game. First off, here are some rules I have for this game. Most of these rules are unsaid or instead discouraged, but since this is a newcomers game I'll restate and enforce these rules for this game here. 1. You are expected to follow every Game Rule found in #rules. 2. Always play for your own faction's win. 3. Do not attempt to encrypt messages in order to communicate with another player. 4. Do not use any form of provable randomness. This includes using Discord bots where any other player can see it, screenshots of RNG websites, etc. Saying you used RNG is fine, as long as there isn't any proof that you did so. 5. Do not copy and paste or screenshot anything from your player chat. Paraphrasing is fine. 6. The Stupid Rule: If anything I feel you are doing is an attempt to circumvent or create a loophole for any of the rules, I will take what I feel is appropriate action. 7. Have fun! This is meant to be a casual game and a learning experience. Here are some general mechanics which will be important to know while playing this game. - Day phases will be 48h, Night phases will be 24h. - A private mafia chat will be open at all times during the game. - You must send 25 messages every 24 hours during the day phase or you will be prodded. 3 prods will result in a replacement. If a day ends due to a majority vote before 24 hours have passed, there will be no prods. - Majority will be enabled, meaning if over half the players vote a player, the day will end immediately. - Plurality will also be enabled, meaning if 48 hours have passed during the day with no one having received a majority of the votes, the player with the most votes will be lynched and the day will end. - If half the players (rounded up if odd amount) vote "No Lynch", then nobody will be lynched that day, and the day will end immediately. - If a vote is tied, it will lead to a no vote. Here is the Discord Mafia page that shows how this setup operates, role cards, and role mechanics: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/NewD3 If you have any questions that aren’t in here or in the wiki article, please don’t be afraid to ask Lune or me. One last thing that’s just a personal pet peeve of mine before the setup and roles are rolled and given out: Please don’t lynch new players for acting weird early on, odds are it is explained by them being new and not them being evil. It’s meant to be a learning experience and they can’t learn if they die off the bat. With that, everything will be rolled and roles will be received soon. (edited)
