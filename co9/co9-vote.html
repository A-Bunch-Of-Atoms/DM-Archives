Plurality voting: Enabled Majority voting: Enabled No Lynch: Enabled, requires the majority to vote it and will end the day immediately if hammered. If you need to look up any of the above terms, consult this helpful wiki article: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/Voting_Terminology To vote another player type /vote [Name]. To unvote type /unvote You can change your vote to another player by typing /vote [Name] again, you do not have to unvote to do that. There will be regular "vote counters" which we, the hosts, will post for you where you can check up on who is currently voting! If you vote for someone not in the game, or nonsense votes, they will be counted as self votes. If you have a day action that has to be used in a public chat, then make sure to ping me when using it. Only day actions that you ping me with will be eligible ones, anything else can be considered fake. If you fake a day action and ping me I'll slaughter you.
