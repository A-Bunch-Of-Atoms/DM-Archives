Phases - The game starts with a day phase (Day One). - Day Phases: 48 hours. - Night Phases: 24 hours. Mafia/Mason - Private chats are 24/7. - The Mafia's faction kill is enforced, not choosing a target will result in an RNG kill. - The Mafia Night 1 Rolecop may multitask should the Goon die day 1.
