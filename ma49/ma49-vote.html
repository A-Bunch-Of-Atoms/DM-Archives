Voting Explanation @Alive line MA This will explain how voting works and how you vote other players. Plurality voting: Enabled Majority voting: Enabled starting Day 2 No Lynch: Enabled starting Day 2, requires the majority to vote it and will end the day immediately if hammered. If you need to look up any of the above terms, consult this helpful wiki article: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/Voting_Terminology To vote another player type /vote [Name]. To unvote type /unvote You can change your vote to another player by typing /vote [Name] again, you do not have to unvote to do that. There will be regular "vote counters" which we, the hosts, will post for you where you can check up on who is currently voting! If you vote for someone not in the game, or nonsense votes, they will be counted as self votes.
