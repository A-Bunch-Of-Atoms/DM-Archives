~ ~ ~ Welcome to MA75: Mexican Standoff ! ~ ~ ~

Welcome to Mexican Standoff! Hosted by @Ngekop and @Jen, this is a 13P semi-open setup that has at least 5 Town Citizens and 3 town PR’s (including Cop and Jailkeeper). This is similar to Matrix14 in the sense that the remaining roles will be determined by picking a row, column or main diagonal of a 5x5 table (in Matrix14 it was from a 6x6). That will also determine the possibility of a second faction (Werewolf) making it 2 factions of 2 members each (if this isnt the case, there will be exactly 3 mafias) or the chance of a serial killer appearing as well!

Setup

+ 5 Citizens + 1 Jailkeeper + 1 Cop - 1 Goon The remaining roles will be chosen by randomly selecting a row, column or main diagonal in the following 5x5 table. Note: if one WW is drawn, it becomes SK; if 2 WW’s are drawn, they mimic the mafia roles on the corresponding setup. To learn more about the setup: https://wiki.mafiascum.net/index.php?title=Mexican_Standoff
Index.php
12:22 p.m.

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Always Enabled Majority: Enabled D2+, Enabled D1 after 24 hours. NoLynch: Enabled D2+ Ties: First to reach. No Votes: No Lynch

Game Mechanics

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Mafia/Werewolf has a 24/7 private chat. Mafia/Werewolf can multitask. The SK is 1-shot notified Bulletproof and chooses between 1 of <Investigation Immune, Ninja, or Strongarm> before the D1 lynch.

Activity Requirements

Prods: At least 25 posts each 24 hours. - These 25 posts must be in relation to the game. - 0 posts in 24h will result in 2 prods. - If you get 3 prods, you will be replaced. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1h+ each day).

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.

Additional Servers

Please join the following server, it is required for this game.
  • https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
Please ensure you are able to potentially join another additional server, such as mafia chat.
