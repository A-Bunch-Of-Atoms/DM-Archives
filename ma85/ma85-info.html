~ ~ ~ Welcome to MA85: Haunted Village! ~ ~ ~

Hosted by @Ngekop and @Hyan, this is a 13p semi-open flavored setup facing the Village against the Undead. 10 towns vs 3 mafias.

Setup

  • Out of the pool of possible roles for each alignment, each role except for Villager and Ghoul (which have a weight of 0) has a weight of 1-3 points.
  • The Village will get a random number between 5 and 9 of ascension points. The Undead will get that number minus 3.
  • The Village are then randomly assigned roles whose combined worth equals their ascension points. The remaining roles will be Villager.
  • The chance for each role to appear is the following:
    • Priest - 25% (1-25)
    • Witch Hunter - 20% (26-45)
    • Sorceress - 15% (46-60)
    • Enchantress - 10% (61-70)
    • Healer - 10% (71-80)
    • Angel - 10% (81-90)
    • Trickster - 10% (91-100)
  • The Undead are told which village roles are in the game and have 24 hours to pick their own powers from their pool (no duplicates allowed). If they fail, the host will randomly assign them powers based on what Village roles exist.
  • The Undead roles in the game are then publicly announced on Day 1. ### Phases
