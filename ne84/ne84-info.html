~ ~ ~ Welcome to NE84: NewD3! ~ ~ ~

Hosted by @Lagavulin with cohost @Ngekop, this balanced semi-open 9p (7 towns vs 2 mafias) setup is popular for newcomer games. Six roles are always the same and the remaining 3 are determined by random rolling against a matrix.

Setup (9p)

+ Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Random Town + Random Town - Goon - Random Mafia The 3 random roles are chosen by rolling one number from 1/2/3 and one letter from A/B/C and using the matrix below. The random town roles will be the ones at the intersection of the letter and number chosen. For example, C3 would mean town gets 2 Masons. The random mafia role will be the second one in the selected letter column. For example, C3 would mean mafia get's a second goon:
