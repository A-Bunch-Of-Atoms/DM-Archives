~ ~ ~ Welcome to Death by Candlelight ~ ~ ~

If the Candle dies, the lights go out, and only the dim glow of a candle remains. Hosted by @Lagavulin, this 13 player setup is a slightly modified version of Death Wind on Mafia GG. The game has a special mechanics for the Town Candle and Mafia Death Caller roles. Setup (13P) + Town Candle + Town Parity Cop + Town Oracle + Town Doctor + Town Citizen + Town Citizen + Town Citizen + Town Citizen + Town Citizen + Town Citizen - Mafia Head - Mafia Death Caller - Mafia Sweeper Click for Role Cards Short PR Descriptions Town Candle: On the day after your death, all votes will be cast anonymously by candlelight. Town Parity Cop: Compare the alignment of who you investigate tonight with who you investigated last night. Once you receive a “different” you can no longer use this ability. Town Oracle: Each night set your crystal ball on player of your choice. If you die, your most recent target’s role is revealed. Town Doctor: Non-consecutive, cannot self-heal. Mafia Head: You appear as town to cop investigations. Mafia Death Caller: Once per game, from day 2 on, in the first 24 hours of day phase, you can kill the player you are currently voting. Mafia Sweeper: Once per game, by eod, you may choose to hide the day execution’s role and alignment from everyone but yourself.
