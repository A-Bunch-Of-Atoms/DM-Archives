For the fifth time, the Circle of Trust has opened. Six spots are open on this time, and it seems the Demons have added a few more rules to the game... how will the Angels adapt? Will the Circle become a safe haven for the fourth time, or will the Demons take back their crown? Game Mechanics - Phases - Nightless - The game only has day phases. Daystart - Resulting of this, the game starts with Day 1. Days - 48 hours - Voting Mechanics - Majority - If a player receives more than half of the available votes, the phase ends with the vote affecting that player. Plurality - The player with the most votes at the end of the day is voted. Ties - Resolved by RNG. NoVotes - Resolved by RNG. - Evil Mechanics - Daychat - The Demons will always have a day chat. Depending on events however, some Demons may be moved from the game. - Activity and Replacement Rules - Activity Requirement - 20-25 messages each 24 hours of a day phase in the MAIN CHAT. Content Requirement - You may also be warned if the message content is insufficient (spam, flufftalk, etc) Warning system - You will be forcefully replaced should you be warned 3 times. Severe Inactitvity - If you ever have no posts after 48 hours you will be forcefully replaced. Setup 13 Players + 10 Angels (Town) - 3 Demons (Mafia) Everyone's win condition is to only send players from your alignment to the Circle of Trust. On Day 1, everyone will vote for a Circle Leader. This person will have more power than the rest of the people in the game, as they will have a vote or the rest of the game no matter what. Between Days 2-6, you will vote for 5 more members to be placed into the Circle of Trust. These members will lose their vote until all 6 people are placed into the Circle. Depending on how many players of each alignment are in the Circle of Trust upon all 6 members entering, 4 different events may occur. Event 1 If no demons were voted into the circle, Town wins! Event 2 If all 3 demons are voted into the circle, Demons win! Event 3 If 1 demon was voted into the circle, all players outside of the circle (plus the Circle Leader) will vote to remove 3 of the players from the circle. If the demon is voted out, 3 new players will be added to the circle. If not, Demons win! If there are no demons in these 3 new players, Town wins! If there are 2 demons in these 3 new players, Demons win! If there is 1 demon in these 3 new players, then these 3 are expelled and the players outside the circle must vote 2 more players into it. After this, the players inside the circle must vote 1 last player in. If the final demon makes it in, Demons win! If the circle is pure, Town wins! Event 4 If 2 demons are voted into the circle, the phase will immediately change. The demons inside of the circle choose to kill someone else inside of the circle, and then the game immediately transitions to a 3v2 (nightless) with the remaining people inside of the circle. If the demons reach parity, Demons win! If town votes out both demons from the circle, Town wins! Good luck! Player Chat Server - https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
