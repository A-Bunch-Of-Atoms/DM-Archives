Voting Explanation

@Alive Line NE

Voting Mechanics

  • Plurality is enabled.
    • This means that the player with the most votes at the end of the day will be eliminated.
  • Majority will be enabled starting day 2.
    • This means, starting day 2, if at any point a player reaches an amount of votes equal to half the alive players +1, they immediately die and the day immediately ends.
  • No-Lynch will be enabled starting day 2.
    • This means, starting day 2, it's possible to end the day without an elimination. If this is voted by the majority, the day immediately ends.
Further Reading:https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/Voting_Terminology

How to Vote

To vote another player type /vote [Name]. To unvote type /unvote You can change your vote to another player by typing /vote [Name] again, you do not have to unvote to do that.
  • There will be regular "vote counters" which we, the hosts, will post for you where you can check up on who is currently voting!
  • If you vote for someone not in the game, or nonsense votes, they will be counted as self votes.
  • Please keep this chat free from discussion and use it solely to vote.
