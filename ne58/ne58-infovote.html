NE58 - D6 Mafia

(13P) One of the most commonly played Mafia Universe setups, which gives an interesting spin to semi-open games.

Setup:

  • 10 Town
  • 3 Mafia
Furthermore, six 6-sided dice will be rolled. Each 1-3 triggers investigative, each 4-5 triggers protective, and each 6 triggers killing. The amount of rolls for a certain type of role decides the role that will be in play. Investigative 1: Innocent Child 2: Voyeur 3: Motion Detector 4: Role Cop 5: Tracker 6: Parity Cop Protective 1: Bodyguard 2: Doctor 3: Roleblocker 4: Jailkeeper 5: Jailkeeper + Bodyguard 6: Roleblocker + Doctor Killing 1: 1-shot Vigilante 2: 2-shot Vigilante 3: Vigilante 4: Vigilante 5: Vigilante + 1-shot Vigilante 6: Vigilante + 2-shot Vigilante Mafia Based on the amount of town PRs, mafia will also get some PRs. If town has 1, mafia gets 0. If town has 2, mafia gets 1. If town has 3, mafia gets 2. One die is rolled for each mafia role. Depending on the roll they get: 1: Mafia Roleblocker 2: Mafia Roleblocker 3: Mafia Jailkeeper 4: Mafia Role Cop 5: Mafia Tracker 6: Mafia Watcher

Game Mechanics:

Day Phase: 48 hours Night Phase: 24 hours Factional Chat: Available 24/7 Factional Kill: Mandatory. If no target is chosen, a random townie will be killed instead.

Voting Mechanics:

Majority: Enabled starting D2 Plurality: Enabled No-Lynch: Enabled starting D2 Flips: Full Alignment/Role Tied Votes: RNG

Activity Requirements:

25 game-related posts are required each 24 hours of a day phase. If you do not reach this goal, you will be prodded. If you receive three prods, you will be force-replaced. Zero posts will result in a double prod. Exceptions to prods/replacements can be made at the mod’s discretion.

Global Rules

All rules not mentioned above that are in #📜rules also apply!
