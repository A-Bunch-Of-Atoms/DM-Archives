@Alive Line SP For clarification, if you have never encountered the "missing" mechanic before, the following generally applies to missing players;
  • Missing players cannot talk or vote anywhere.
  • Missing players cannot be voted.
  • Missing players cannot use actions.
  • Missing players cannot be the target of an action by another player.
