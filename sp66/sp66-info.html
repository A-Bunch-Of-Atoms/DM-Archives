~ ~ ~ Welcome to...~ ~ ~

The Rise of the Cult

Hosted by @wlwillow and @Danger!? Luka!

Phases

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours. Nightskip is always enabled. Starting Phase: Day 1. There is a Day 1 Execution. Action Lock: 30m before Day breaks

Voting

Majority: Enabled Plurality: Enabled No Execution: Disabled Abstaining: The Town loses. Ties: Random Elimination among the tied players. No Execution will always win ties.

Game Mechanics

Cause of Death: Off. There are no nightkills in this game. Flips: Full. Feedback: None. Last Wills: Off. There are no last wills. See Setup for more.

Activity

- 15 messages are required every 24 hours. Failing to meet this requirement will result in a prod. - No posting for 24 hours results in 2 prods. - 3 prods and you will be forcefully replaced.

Setup - 11 Players

ansi x10 Vanilla Town x1 Cult Leader The Cult convert a player each night as a faction instead of killing. The Cult win automatically after Day 3 is over, but if the Town kills even a single Cultist, the Town instantly wins the game. There is no difference between the Cult and their Leader, other than flavor.

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.

This setup is conversion-based. There will be midgame conversion.

Additional Servers

Please join the following server, it is required for this game.
  • https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
