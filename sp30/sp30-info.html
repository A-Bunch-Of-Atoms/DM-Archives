Each universe begins with half of the players in the game (2 Villains, 5 Heroes, 1 Researcher.) Dying at night in any universe will send you to the other universe. This will not reveal your role. Lynching a player will do the same, but will reveal the player's alignment. Lynching an non-Hero will reveal their role. After 4 days, the game will end. Villains have a private chat shared with all other Villains. Villains must perform one factional kill per universe. The factional kill cannot be used alongside a player's abilities. Villains may factional kill their teammates, but they cannot factional kill themself. Villains win if there are 3 or more Villains in any universe at the end of the game. Heroes win if there are 2 Villains in each universe at the end of the game. Researchers win if they have more points than the other researcher. Both researchers lose if they have the same amount of points.
