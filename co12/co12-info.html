Disclaimer: This game is meant to be taken seriously, activity requirements are exceptionally high. Disclaimer 2: This will be a test run of the Episode format, a more competitive setup, there might be imbalance, and we are here to find it. Please report anything you find to require change. Mechanics and Information

- Phases - Day - 48 hours Night - 24 Hours Daystart - The game starts on a day phase (Day 1) - Voting - Plurality - Enabled Majority - Enabled D2 Nolynch - Enabled D2 Ties - Broken by who reached the tie first. Novotes - If nobody is voted at natural EOD then a random player will be eliminated. - Feedback - Night Feedback - You will learn what happens to you at night, such as you being roleblocked, or attacked. Kill Feedback - Cause of death/the killer will not be revealed. There will be flavour. Default Feedback - The default feedback if no feedback is received will be "You Slept Peacefully." - Evil Mechanics - Daytalk - Enabled, the evil faction may converse at all times. Mafia Multitasking - Disabled, a Mafia member is required to sacrifice their action in order to factionally kill. Randomized Evils - The evil team is randomly decided pre-game, the players will not be informed which team rolled. - Activity and Replacement Rules - Activity Requirement - 30-35 messages each 24 hours of a day phase in the MAIN CHAT. Content Requirement - You may also be warned if the message content is insufficient (spam, flufftalk, etc) Warning system - You will be forcefully replaced should you be warned 3 times. Severe Inactitvity - If you ever have no posts after 48 hours you will be forcefully replaced. Replacements - Each slot may only see one replacement, after which it is modkilled instead.
