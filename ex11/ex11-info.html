EX10 - Testing the Masterlist

This game will test #gim-master-list, an alternative GIM thread which means to compile the best entries in all of #gim-anarchy, #gim-season-1 and #gim-season-2. Currently only #gim-anarchy and the beginnings of #gim-season-1 have been added, so the list will be a bit restrictive. Future games running this list will have a lot more chaos to offer.
  • The master list only has town, mafia, coven and neutral roles.

Testing Purpose

The main purpose of this test is to see if the idea actually works in the sense that the game feels "more balanced" than your usual GIM experience, now that the list will be somewhat curated. Technically speaking this would also open the door for set slot lists (like a semi open)? God knows I'd like to hear some feedback about this after the game. :)

Special Game Mechanics

  • Flipless effects (Clean/Stone/etc) will simply go by "role unknown" and the killer/faction will not be revealed.
  • There will be a mandatory Dead Chat, you have to be on it after death until the game ends, participating in it however is optional.
  • Mafia Espionage will let the mafia know that there is a Mafia Espionage role per Mafia Espionage rolled (unless the rolecard says otherwise).
    • I don't think any exist at the moment so w/e
  • Mafia & Coven will be limited to 4 members, 5 if 25+ players, 6 if 40+, espionage roles are excluded.
  • There may only be 1 of Mafia Head and Coven Head.
  • There may only be 2 Town Power.
  • Town is not guaranteed to have the majority of players at game start.
  • The role list will be entirely random except if one role of a faction (except Neutral) rolls, I guarantee there to be at least one more of that faction.
    • Coven has very little roles and may not appear at all in this first masterlist game!
