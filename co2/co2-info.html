Welcome! Welcome to the grand opening of our doors for the second Community game! Hosted by @Lunexia on Osu! and yours truly! You have voted, and we deliver! Throne of Lies it shall be! This second iteration of our community games has been voted into existence by you, the community sometime last year as we asked if you would like to see a Discord Mafia edition of the popular steam social deduction game Throne of Lies. As many of you were interested, it is finally here! As mentioned, this game will be mostly just like Throne of Lies. I recommend familiarizing yourself with their wiki if you have never heard of it, as it holds the roles and various mechanics. I will not go over roles and specific mechanics here, but what I will do is explain the basics. The Basics First off, here's the wiki: https://tol.fandom.com/wiki/Throne_of_Lies:_The_Online_Game_of_Lies_and_Deceit_Wikia Throne of lies is played similar to Town of Salem, or Mafia. But there is one big difference, the evil faction that replaces the Mafia will be able to convert town members over the course of the game until they lose their leader. This means that you can never fully trust anyone but yourself. Additionally, as you have decided by vote, which faction the evils actually belong to will be unknown! This is because Throne of Lies features two evil factions, the Cult and the Unseen, which both work similarly in that they can convert town members, but are different by design. Only one of them will be in the game however. Additionally, there will be a King in the game, the King is publicly known, but the King can belong to any alignment between Blue Dragon (Town) and Cult/Unseen (Mafia) at the start of the game, but should the King die a new one is crowned among the remaining players. I recommend reading up on the King mechanics on the wiki here: https://tol.fandom.com/wiki/The_King The role list will have certain roles and types of roles hardrolled into the games, and some more should the ones rolled first force them to roll.
