~ ~ ~ Welcome to CO16: The Godfather! ~ ~ ~

The Godfather has died! There now exists a power struggle within the mafia. Will you become the head of the ORGanization?

Setup - Up to 20 players!

Agent Ambusher Beguiler Blackmailer Clerk Consigliere Consort Courier Disguiser Forger Framer Goon Hypnotist Janitor Kidnapper Mafioso Mortician Prankster Town Traitor Vandal All "roles" have no special abilities, this is just for flavor. Everyone starts off the same.

Phase Mechanics

Challenge: 24 Hours Voting: 24 Hours Phase Start Time: 1:00 pm EST Starting Phase: Challenge The game will start off with the players split up into two teams competing against each other. At some point, the teams will be put one team.

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Always Enabled Ties: Revote between the tied players. If tied again, then will be a random draw among players who aren't part of the tie and aren't immune. Vote Submission: Submit votes in player chat. Votes will be revealed anonymously!

Activity Requirements

Prods: No prods, but it's very recommended to post in ORGs, in the main chat and in DMs! Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it. Replacements aren't exactly great for this type of game. Being replaced will result in signup bans.

Phase Mechanics

Challenge: During the Challenge phase, you will receive a challenge that depending on the phase of the game, you will complete either with your team or individually. If you/your team wins, you will receive immunity and/or some other potential benefit. During the team portion of the game, if your team wins immunity, you will not have to vote anyone off. During the individual portion of the game, if you win immunity, you will be unable to be voted off. Voting: During the Voting phase, you will cast a vote on another player, and the player with the most votes is eliminated. However, during this, players will have the opportunity to play any advantages they may have to try and alter the vote in ways such as nullifying votes cast on them, gaining access to multiple votes, and more. At the end, one person has to be eliminated, and the player with the most votes that count is eliminated.

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.

Additional Servers
