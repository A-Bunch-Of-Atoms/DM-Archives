NE57 - Elimination Games

(9P) A (slightly) unique game ran by @sunny based off of a MafiaScum setup where the town wins if a mafia is eliminated on Day 3.

Setup:

  • 7 Town Citizen
  • 2 Mafia Goon

Game Mechanics:

Day Phase: 48 hours long Night Phase: 24 hours Factional Chat: Available 24/7 Factional Kill: Mandatory. If no target is chosen, a random townie will be killed instead. Desperation Day: Begins and ends on Day 3. If a Mafia is eliminated on this day, the Town wins. Flavor: The flavor of this game has some interactive gameplay! It is optional and will not affect the game. This will be discussed more thouroughly when the game begins. Rolecards: #deleted-channel

Voting Mechanics:

Majority: Enabled on D2 Plurality: Enabled No-Lynch: Disabled Flips: Full Alignment/Role Tied Votes: RNG No Votes: Random Lynch

Activity Requirements:

25 game-related posts are required each 24 hours of a day phase. If you do not reach this goal, you will be prodded. If you receive three prods, you will be force-replaced. Zero posts will result in a double prod. Exceptions to prods/replacements can be made at the mod’s discretion.

Global Rules

All rules not mentioned above that are in #📜rules also apply!
