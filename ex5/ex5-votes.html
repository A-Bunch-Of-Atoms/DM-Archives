How to Vote You can vote other players by typing: /vote [playername] You can stop voting that player by typing: /unvote You may switch to another player just by typing the same as in the first example with the different name, unvoting is not required. You can vote for a no-lynch by typing: /vote nolynch What do Majority and stuff mean? Majority: If someone is voted by more than half the amount of players alive in the game, then they are instantly voted out. During day one, where 9 players are alive, the majority is 5. So if somoene is voted by 5 other players, they're out. A majority being reached also ends a day phase early. Example: Majority is 5. (Could be seen in a votec ounter.) Plurality: This means that the person with the most votes at the end of each day is voted out. This happens if no majority was reached and the day ended naturally. No lynch: Voting for no lynch is something you do strategically, or when you do not have any good options for players to lynch that day. It is generally always better to lynch anyway, because you always have a chance to vote out a mafia member, but some situations will leave this as a viable option if you need it. During this game, you cannot do this on the first day phase. @Alive Line CO If you have not yet played before, the above should explain pretty in depth on how you can vote each other. :)
