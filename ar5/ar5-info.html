Seven Wonders (New Edition)

A Game by Antoine Bauza, for 4 to 7 players

Summary

Seven Wonders is a drafting-based strategy game, where players will select one card from the hand they receive, choose what to do with it, and then pass the hand on to the next player in sequence. The game is divided into three phases (called Ages), each with their own set of cards, and will aim to build resources, develop their commerce and military, score points with civilian and scientific projects, and finally earn their own unique bonuses by building their eponymous Wonder. Who will finish with the grandest city of all when the three Ages are complete?

Full Rulebook

The complete set of game rules are detailed below, along with an image summary of the list of cards available in the full 7-player setup of the game. If fewer than 7 slots are filled, then the deck will be smaller (changes will be outlined before the start of the game) https://cdn.svc.asmodee.net/production-rprod/storage/downloads/games/7wonders/en/sev-en02-rules-1716388903zBEZx.pdf

Procedure

The game has a maximum of 7 slots but I will permit 2-person hydras to play as one slot if there is interest for more than 7 people to play. Players will be able to talk in a public chat, but will submit their choices in secret in their respecting player-chats. Collusion between players outside of the public chat is strictly not permitted! Moves will be processed as soon as all players have chosen an action in their player chats; there will be a soft deadline of 24 hours per move, if a slot repeatedly takes much longer than that to submit an action, I may replace them at my discretion. There are 6 turns per Age, with one turn in the Pregame for players to choose which side of their wonder they wish to play - the whole game should therefore not take more than three weeks to complete!
