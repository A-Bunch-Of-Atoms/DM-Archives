Mechanics

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Voting: Everyday players will vote on who to exicute for the day (or to to exicute no one starting day 2). At the end of the day who ever has the most votes will be exicuted, in the result of a tie; the person who reached the the vote count first will be exicuted. If a player reachs majority at any point the day will end and they will be exicuted. Flips: On death players with have their role reviled. Mafia: Mafia has multitask(can kill and use an ability) and may talk in private at any time. Feedback: Low. Only Traker, Cop, Neopolition, and Rolecop will get feedback from their abilities. All players will be told that they "Slept Peacfuly" or that they died.
