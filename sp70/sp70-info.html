Mission 2 - Hanabi Mini

In this game you will be aiming to play a set of different coloured and numbered cards in order. There are cards of 5 different colours and numbered 1 to 4, with the numbers having to be played in ascending order Each team will be playing one hand of five cards, however you may only view your opposite team's cards. On each turn, the team whose turn it is may play one of their cards, discard one of their cards, or give a clue to the opposite team. When you play a card, as long as the card is the next number in sequence of its respective colour, it will score a point for your team. If you score a total of 16 points, then the mission will be a success! If the card is not the next in sequence in its colour, it will count as a misfire. If you misfire 3 times then the mission will fail! After a card is played or discarded, you will draw a new card from the deck, always into your leftmost slot in your hand. All other cards to the left of the card that was played/discarded will move to the right one slot. Clues may take the form of a colour, or a number; this will reveal which your opposite team's cards that fit that category. As a whole, you have 8 clues in total which you can give; however discarding a card always earns one extra clue. The game will also end if it is a team's turn to draw a card, but there are none remaining in the deck. The mission proper will start 2025-06-28 1:00 p.m. , after which you have 48 hours to complete your objective. Mafia chat as well as the main chat will be locked, and you will be given two new chats in the playerchat server in your respective teams. (edited)
