NE94 - Five of Hearts

Hosted by @sunny. A semi-open 9p game (7v2) with a minor storyline. Setup - 9p + Town Citizen x5 - Mafia Goon The three remaining roles will be picked by randomly choosing a letter (A/B/C) AND a number (1/2/3) to give one of 9 specific setups to be played (image below). The letter will select the Mafia Power Role (Mafia PR), while the cross section of the letter and the number decide the Town Power Role(s) (Town PR). If you wish to learn about this setup, including all possible roles, click here.
12:05 p.m.

Game Mechanics

Day: 48 hours Night: 24 hours Mafia Chat: Available 24/7 Factional Kill: NOT mandatory. The mafia may kill nobody if they so wish. Kill Credit: Enabled Feedback: Feedbackless/No Feedback

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled Majority: Enabled Day 2 No Lynch: Disabled Ties: RNG No Votes: RNG Flips: Full Flips

Activity Requirements

You are required to post at least 25 times each 24 hours, otherwise you'll receive a prod. If you don't post once in a 24h period, you'll receive 2 prods instead. If you receive 3 prods, you'll be arrested and tried in court for being inactive in NE00. (federal crime) Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an good amount of time to it!

Other Info

  • All global rules in #rules apply!
