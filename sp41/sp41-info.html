A stiff winter wind blows through the streets. In the small and ancient republic of Meiningen-Detmold, public life is in decay: potholes litter the roads, trams never seem to run on time, the rent is out of control and the emergency services are on their knees. Can the good citizens see through their lies and eliminate their nefarious influence? Or will Democracy die in daylight?! MEET THE MAFIA - Paralyzer Once per game, during the Day, may lock the town vote, meaning that any player that has already voted during the Day may not change their vote. -Ballot Stuffer Once per game, during the Evening phase (at the end of the Day), may overturn the vote to the player with the second highest vote count. May not overturn the vote if the player with the second highest vote count is being voted solely by members of the Mafia. If there are players who are tied for second, may choose between any of them, provided that the overturn follows the above rule. -Silovik Once per game, during the Evening phase (at the end of the Day), may cause all players with a minority vote on them (i.e. at least one vote) in the town vote to be lynched in place of the player who would otherwise be lynched. Power may not be used if the player with the highest number of votes is a member of the Mafia. Power may not be used if the town has voted for a no-lynch, or there is a tie for first place in the town vote. -Political Hack Once per game, during the Day or during Evening phase (at the end of the Day), may choose to alter the results that the pollster would receive upon using their ability to determine what proportion of the day's votes were on mafia. May use their power to alter any day's results up to and including the game's current Day. If due to be lynched at the end of the Day phase, may not act during the subsequent Evening Phase. Must specify a percentage to the nearest .1%.

MEET THE TOWN +Pollster Once per game, during the Night, may conduct a poll on any of the previous town final votecounts, including the Day just passed, and receive a report that tells them what percentage of the votes cast were for Mafia. Result received will be a percentage with precision to the nearest .1% Example: if 8 votes were cast and 5 of them were for players aligned with the Mafia on the Day in question, their report would tell them that 62.5% of votes cast were for Mafia. +Coumminity Leader If killed during the Night, then the following Day's town vote will be anonymous.
