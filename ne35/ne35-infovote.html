Welcome to NewD3, the best newcomer setup! For this game, NewD3 will be modified to fit 13p. This features up to 9 known different possible setups, of which one is chosen at random (all of which contain exactly 3 mafias and 1-2 town power roles).
All setups have now 8 town citizens and 2 mafia goons by default. To determine the rest of the setup, a random letter (A / B / C) and a random number (1 / 2 / 3) will be picked. First, the letter will determine the remaining mafia role that is under that column… A: Roleblocker B: Rolecop C: Goon. Then, the cross between the column determined by the letter and the row determined by the number, will determine the two remaining town roles. For example, if setup A1 is rolled, it will be 1 Town Cop, 1 Town Doctor, 8 Town Citizens, 2 Mafia Goons, and 1 Mafia Roleblocker. If you have any questions, please feel free to ask me or Lune. You can read more about the original setup here: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/NewD3
Game Mechanics Day - 48 Hours Night - 24 Hours Daystart Mafia chat - 24/7 Plurality - Always enabled (meaning if 48 hours have passed during the day with no one receiving majority of the votes, the player with the most votes will be lynched and the day will end). Majority - Always enabled (meaning if over half the players vote a same player, said player will be lynched and day will end immediately). No Lynch - Enabled Day 2+ (meaning if at least half the players vote “No Lynch”, no one will be lynched and day will end immediately; also, if after 48 hours there is at least a “No Lynch” vote and no player has more votes than “No Lynch”, no one will be lynched either and day will end). Ties - In case after 48 hours 2 or more players are tied with most votes, whoever reached that amount of votes definitely first will get lynched. No votes - RNG. Night Feedback - Feedbackless, meaning you will not learn if anything happened to you at night. Prodding - Minimum 20 messages every 24 hours. Getting prodded with 0 messages will result in a double prod. If you get 3 prods, you will be forced replaced. Once the game fills, I will set up the game and deal rolecards in your playerchats. Once you receive your rolecard, please confirm you have seen and understood it by giving back your alignment, what your roles abilities are as well as what your win condition is. You will have 24 hours for this.
If you fail to do this, you will be force replaced before the game starts.
