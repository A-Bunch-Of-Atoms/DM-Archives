~ ~ ~ Welcome to NE50: Mason9er! ~ ~ ~

Ran by @Ngekop and @charlie it’s an open 9p setup featuring masons, two town members having a private chat and acknowledging each other as town!

Setup

- 2 Town Mason - 5 Town Citizen - 2 Mafia Goon

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled D2+ - If a player is voted by more than half of the currently living players, the day immediately ends with that player’s death. NoLynch: Disabled Ties: Resolved by RNG - If two or more players are tied in most votes when the day ends, a random one is chosen to be eliminated. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is eliminated.

Private Chat and Game Mechanics

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Mafia and masons have a 24/7 private chat each. Mafia must kill someone every night; if no target is submitted, a random town player will be killed.

Activity Requirements

You are required to post at least 20 times each 24 hours of a day phase or you will get prodded. If you get 3 prods, you will be forced replaced. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. - You are required to post these 20 messages in relation to the game. - 0 posts in 24h will result in a double prod. Please only sign up if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1h+ per day).

Rules

Make sure to have read the Game Rules so you don't accidentally break one of them. Breaking our rules can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.

Additional Servers

Please ensure being able to potentially join other additional servers, such as mafia/mason chat.
