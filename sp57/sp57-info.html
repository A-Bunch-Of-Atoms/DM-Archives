MAP MAFIA - CAMBRIAN KINGDOMS

A game for 16-21 players hosted by @Ngekop and @kirigiri In this game, the players will find themselves scattered across the hills and valleys of Wales; with the town vying to protect themselves and their pastoral home against the clutches of the Mafia! Location and movement will be a key component of gameplay in this setup, as the actions of both factions will be informed and restricted by where players find themselves on the map. Universal Rules: Day phase Length: 48 hours Night phase Length: 24 hours (split into two parts of 12 hours each) Mafia Multitasking: Enabled Feedback: Minimal; players will not automatically know if their protective or killing actions were successful. Role Reveal on Death: Full roles revealed by default Voting Plurality: Always Enabled Voting Majority: Enabled from Day 2 onwards No-lynch: Enabled from Day 2 onwards Tiebreaking: Handled by Priority No votes: Resolved by RNG
5:04 p.m.
Map-related Rules: Each player has an associated location, or 'tile' on the map. During the Night phases, both town and mafia may opt to move. By default, town players move in the first half of the Night, and mafia players move in the second half. Town players' default maximum move is set at 4 tiles. Mafia by default may move a maximum of 5. In order for mafia to perform their factional kill, at the end of their movement the killing mafia player must be on or adjacent to the tile that their target occupies. During the Day, players that occupy the same tile will share a neighhbourhood chat, and all players will be able to see the locations of players that are adjacent to them. At the start of the game, town players will be each assigned a random tile in which they start the game. No two town players can start in the same location. The mafia start the game informed of the town's starting locations and can choose where they deploy. The mafia may also designate three tiles to serve as 'observation posts'. If a any living mafia occupies one of these tiles, the faction will have the entire map and players revealed to them. The Bomb: After the mafia have been deployed, the location of a 'bomb' will also be randomly assigned on the map. This bomb cannot be on or adjacent to any of the mafia's starting locations, and is not revealed by any 'observation post'. Any mafia player passing through a tile on or adjacent to the 'bomb' will have its location revealed to them, and at the end of their movement can opt to pick it up. Beginning at their next eligible movement phase, the mafia player carrying bomb may, independent of their factional kill, opt to detonate the bomb, attacking any players on or adjacent to the tile in which it is detonated, including the bomb's carrier.
5:05 p.m.
Player Roles: Every player at the start of the game will receive a unique role. This role is given agnostic as to whether the player is town or mafia. The list of roles will not be given publicly, but hosts will attempt to keep the setup balanced! All else that is known is that there are 16 town and 5 mafia! (edited)
