Welcome to Jungle Republic

This game will feature two anti-town factions, both the Mafia and the Werewolves, however, they both work differently as in that one can kill, while the other cannot, making their gamplay quite different from each other. Additionally, the Town posesses an investigative role able to find the Anti-Town faction that CAN kill. The following will be the rules, mechanics as well as the setup.

Setup

https://wiki.mafiascum.net/index.php?title=Jungle_Republic

Role List

- 3x Mafia Goons - 2x Werewolves - 6x Town Citizen - 1x Town Seer

Game Mechanics

  • The Seer receives Werewolf/Not Werewolf as the investigation result.
  • The Mafia or Werewolves win if they obtain a full majority of living players, or if they make up half and at least one Town role is still alive.
  • If the Mafia and Werewolves ever make up equal numbers, with no Town roles left alive, then mafia win. Mafia also win if a day phase begins with one Mafia, one Werewolf, and one Town.

Phases

Starting Phase: Daystart Day: 72 Hours Night: 24 Hours Day Starting Time: At 16 UTC0 -> 16 UTC 0 = 12:00 p.m. in your timezone

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 72 hours will die. Majority: Enabled starting day 2. - If a player is voted by half of the currently living players +1 then the day immediately ends with that players death. NoLynch: Enabled starting day 2 - If this option has the most votes at the end of the day then nobody dies. If this option is voted by half of the living players +1, then the day immedaitely ends with nobody dying. Ties: Resolved by RNG - If two or more players are tied in votes when the day ends, a random one is chosen to be eliminated. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is eliminated.

Anti-Town Mechanics

Mafia has a 24/7 chat. Mafia does not have a night kill. Werewolves have a 24/7 chat. Werewolves have a night kill.

Activity Requirements

Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 25 times each 24 hours of a day phase. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 25 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. Replacements: Each slot may only see one replacement total, after which it will instead be modkilled if another replacement becomes necessary. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1 hour or more each day).

Questions

If you have any questions about the setup (as it is quite unusual) let me know please.

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them. Breaking our rules can lead to punishments, such as game bans, or forceful replacement by a different player, which I would like to avoid if possible.

Additional Servers

Please make sure you have enough space on your Discord to join up to two additional servers for this game.
  • Please join the following server, it is required for this game.
    • https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
  • Please ensure you are able to potentially join another additional servers, such as a mafia chat.
