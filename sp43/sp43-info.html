AIFAM ESREVER - DISCORD EDITION (15-20p)

Welcome to Reverse Mafia! Hosted by @Ngekop and @kirigiri, this setup features a scum team that has majority and has to hunt down a town that will try to slowly increase their numbers while killing scum each night, as well as an Alien attempting to steal the win from both sides Notice that there are conversion mechanics with traitors being potentially turned into masons, but also there are tracker conversion mechanics which allows the tracker to switch to a vigilante or a masonizer in specific events to allow town to keep killing and/or converting if any of the original roles in charge of these dies! Setup - Rolecop - Goon - Traitor — 9-14 + Masonizer + Tracker + Vigilante = Alien Phases Daystart Day: 48 hours Night: 24 hours Voting Mechanics Plurality: Always enabled. - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled starting day 2. - If a player is voted by half of the currently living players +1 then the day immediately ends with that players death. NoLynch: Enabled starting day 2 - If this option has the most votes at the end of the day then nobody dies. If this option is voted by half of the living players +1, then the day immedaitely ends with nobody dying. Ties: Resolved by RNG - If two or more players are tied in votes when the day ends, a random one is chosen to be lynched. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is lynched. Mafia Mechanics - Mafia has a private 24/7 chat. - Mafia can decide to no kill but may not multitask! Mason Mechanics - Masons have a private 24/7 chat. This includes the Masonizer. - If the Masonizer's Night conversion target is a member of the Mafia, all currently living Masons, including the Masonizer, will die that Night, and the target will remain unconverted. - If the Masonizer dies in a manner excluding the above effect, and there is at least one living Mason, then a Mason will be randomly selected from this pool to be converted to Masonizer immediately after the previous Masonizer dies. The Alien may not become the Masonizer in this way. Tracker Conversion Mechanics - Masonizer becomes an eligible conversion target if and only if the role is currently out of the game. Tracker may not covert to Masonizer if any player currently holds that role, even if the original Masonizer is dead. - If the Tracker converts to Masonizer, then a new Mason group is formed that is separate from the original group; this new group of Masons share a separate chat and do not have access to the chatlogs of the original group. - Vigilante becomes an eligible conversion target if and only if the player who began the game as the Vigilante is dead. - If the Vigilante was converted to Mason before their death, then the Tracker will be informed of the original Vigilante's role upon their death. Activity Requirements Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 20 times each 24 hours, otherwise you'll receive a prod. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. - If you don't post once in a 24h period, you'll receive 2 prods instead. - If you receive 3 prods, you'll be arrested and sent to jail and confined in a cell with how and kirin yang for up to 28 days, whereafter you may be charged and tried for ISP with malicious intent. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 20 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1 hour or more each day).

Role Descriptions - Rolecop May choose a player and learn their role every night. - Goon A member of the Mafia without special abilities. - Traitor Has no sepecial abilities, but wins with the Mafia instead of the town. Does not count toward the total number of Mafia for purposes of reaching a majority. + Masonizer May choose a player to be converted to Mason every night + Tracker May choose a player and learn who they visited every night. In cases where the Masonizer or Vigilante are dead, may choose instead to use their night action to convert to Vigilante or Masonizer (see Tracker Conversion Mechanics). + Vigilante May shoot and eliminate a player every night. = Alien Each night chooses a player to 'probe'. This action does not appear as a visit to the Tracker. If at the end of the game (i.e. at the point where the Mafia or the Town would otherwise achieve their win condition), every living player has been probed by the Alien, the Alien instead wins alone. May not be killed in the night by either the Mafia or the Vigilante. If chosen by the Masonizer as the Mason target, joins the Mason meeting as if they were a Mason, but retains their original win condition. (edited)
