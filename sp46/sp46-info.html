Phase Mechanics

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Day Start Time: 9:00 EST Starting Phase: Day 1. There is a Day 1 Execution.

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Always Enabled Majority: Enabled D2+ Skipping: Enabled Abstaining: Random Elimination among all living players. Ties: Random Elimination among the tied players.

Lords & Elections

Elections: On Day 2 and every phase after a Lord dies, a Lord election takes place, but not if there is 4 or less players alive. Timing: The Lord election lasts 24 hours, until every player votes, or when hammer is reached. You may still vote normally during a Lord election. Voting Format: Vote IN YOUR PLAYER CHANNEL. Crowning: Whoever receives the most votes will be crowned Lord and receive that card (usually evil players retain their original role, too.). If votes are tied, another election will be held the following day.

Other Mechanical Information

Flips: Roles are revealed on death Kill Feedback: None. Night Feedback: Stated on the document. Charges: Charges are always consumed, even if the ability fails. Evil Chat: The Unshackled/Cult will have a 24/7 chat. The Azure Dragoon will need to defeat any Neutral Evils added to scum chat alongside the Unshackled/Cult. Factional Kill: The factional kill is assigned and multitaskable. Neutral Evils may perform the factional kill if they are in scum chat.

Activity Requirements

Prods: At least 25 posts each 24 hours. Failure to meet this requirement will result in a "Prod". - These 25 posts must be in relation to the game. - If you get 2 prods, you will be replaced. Day 1 flavor

It is a glorious day in the region. The sun is shining, birds are sinding, and the local Lord is giving a speech in celebration of the Summer Solstice. "Friends compatriots, my fellow Anglanders.." she drolls on for what feels like hours on end. After she finishes her speech, mild applause is heard from the audience. She turns to leave off the stage... but not before a crossbow bolt is fired directly into her heart. It comes from the Marquess' box. The Marquess is the adpotive child of the Lord; why would they kill their own mother? A hooded figure stands in the box, with the Marquess seemingly nowhere to be seen. "I am the Marquess! I have letters proving the Lord's allegiance to forces unknown! They have conspired to rule Angland with an iron fist; and I will have none of it!" The hooded figure runs off, without their face being known, and scatters letters all over the floor. Upon inspection, the letters do, indeed, prove that the Lord was conspiring; but they do not state with whom. Revolutionaries? Or blood cult? Only time will tell.
