Vote Explanation @Alive line MA This will explain how voting works and how you vote other players. With 19 players alive at the start of the game, a player needs to be voted by 10 players for a hammer to occur. Majority voting: Enabled Plurality voting: Enabled No Lynch: Enabled If you need to look up any of the above terms, consult this helpful wiki article: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/Voting_Terminology To vote another player type /vote [Name]. To unvote type /unvote You can change your vote to another player by typing /vote [Name] again, you do not have to unvote to do that. Voting is done in this channel. Please only use this channel for voting and keep conversations in #ma36-chat. There will be regular "vote counters" which I will post for you where you can check up on who is currently voting!
