Hello everyone. I cooked up a TCG Mafia game after being inspired by @Guillo 's Deck Mafia. You draw cards, which reflect your actions, you play them to use their effects. Effects can range from a simple heal to a permanent bastard transport that transports the targets every night without feedback until they die. I hope you will have fun with this, it will be a nightmare to host. xD Pre game draw Each town player starts without any cards. Each mafia player starts with one scum card. Auto Draw Each player draws a normal card at the start of each odd night (Night 1, 3, 5, etc). This cannot be stopped, unless their hand is full, in which case this fails. Play Action Each player may play one card each phase, should they have a card that they can play during the given phase. The amount of playable cards may change depending on cards you have. Draw Action A member of the Town may, once per game, decide to draw a normal card at night, this cannot be stopped and will replace any other actions they could have taken otherwise. A member of the Mafia may draw a normal card as well as a dedicated scum card once per game at night, this cannot be stopped and will replace any other actions they could have taken otherwise. In either case, if the player has a full hand at the end of the night, this action fails and will not be refunded. The card drawn is a random card either of the normal or scum card pool. Hand size limitation and forced discard Each player has a hand size of 4, if they draw a fifth or more cards then the draw fails. Setup is mountainous. + 12 Citizen - 5 Mafioso + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen - Traitor - Mafioso - Mafioso - Mafisoo - Mafioso Mafia Kill The Mafia has a bonus action. They can choose one of their members which then uses an attack as a bonus action (they can play cards alongside it). Traitor - Mafia doesnt know who he is and he doesnt know who the mafia is. - Draws cards in the same fashion as mafia. - Will know once he is the last mafia alive and may perform the factional kill each night from there. Game Stuff Day: 48 Night: 24 Mafia Chat: 24/7 Voting: Plurality: Enabled Majority: Disabled Day 1, Enabled after No-Lynch: Disabled Day 1, Enabled after Feedback: You will learn what cards were added/removed from your hand. You will receive feedback for cards you play. You will receive "You Slept Peacefully" if literally nothing happened. Flips: Roles flip by default. Death Cause doesn't flip. Prods: 15-20 posts each 24 hours minimum. You get 2 warnings. Replacements: Each slot may only see one replacement. A replacement will be instantly modkilled if they get prodded immediately after replacing into the game. Reminders - Mafia will be extra hard to play, since you need to fake a believable flow of actions where you hide when you used/drew scum cards.
