~ ~ ~ Welcome to NE65: Cop9er! ~ ~ ~

Hosted by @Ngekop and @kirigiri, it’s a semi-vanilla open 9p (7 towns vs 2 mafias) setup that features only one power role…the Cop! The following will be the rules, mechanics as well as the setup.

Phases

Starting Phase: Daystart Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours

Setup

- 1 Cop - 6 Citizens - 2 Mafia Goons

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled starting day 2. - If a player is voted by more than half of the currently living players then the day immediately ends with that player’s death. NoLynch: Enabled starting day 2 - If this option has the most votes at the end of the day then nobody dies. If this option is voted by over half the players then the day immedaitely ends with nobody dying. Ties: First to reach the tied number of votes. - If two or more players are tied in most votes when the day ends, the first one that reached the number of votes they tied at (without it dropping down after that at any moment), will be lynched. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is lynched.

Game Mechanics

- Mafia has a 24/7 private chat. - Mafia has a mandatory factional kill every night.

Activity Requirements

Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 20 times each 24 hours of a day phase or you will get prodded. If you get 3 prods, you will be forced replaced. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 20 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. - If you get prodded by making 0 posts in a 24h period, you will receive 2 prods instead. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1 hour or more each day).

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them. Breaking our rules can lead to punishments, such as game bans, or forceful replacement by a different player, which I would like to avoid if possible.

Additional Servers

Please make sure you have enough space on your Discord to join additional servers for this game.
  • Please join the following server, it is required for this game.
    • https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
  • Please ensure you are able to potentially join another additional servers, such as mafia chat.
