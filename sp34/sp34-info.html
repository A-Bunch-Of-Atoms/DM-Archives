Welcome to Special Game 34 (SP34): Card Game Mafia

The setup I run here will be a creation of my own, featuring over 100 unique cards, similar to a collectible card game, each with their own active or passive effects, some one time, some ongoing, some even more special than that. In addition, in this iteration, we will also have some special roles, to mix things up! AND the card drawing mechanics have been changed heavily for this game as well.

Pre-Game

  • First, every player receives their role.
  • After that, each player is shown 3 boosters with 3 random normal cards, one booster at a time, they have to pick one of the three cards to be shown the next booster. The picked cards will be their cards for this game. Additionally, each mafia player will also be given a fourth booster with 3 scum cards, of which they have to pick one, after opening the first three regular boosters.
    • The Pre-Game phase will last between 24 and 48 hours, depending on how long it takes for every player to go through the boosters to choose their cards.
    • The Mafia will not be allowed to communicate during this phase.

Mafia Draw Action

  • Mafia members may sacrifice their night action to draw a random scum card, they cannot do anything else that night if they do this.
    • A traitor who converted cannot do this.

Hand Size Limitations

Each player is limited to 4 card slots. Drawing a fifth card while 4 cards are already held in hand will make the draw fail.

Role Descriptions

  • Town
    • Dealer: Each night, you may choose a player that's not you, that player will draw a random normal card at the end of the night. (They will learn that a dealer dealt them a card). You start without any cards.
    • Card King: You start without cards, but each night start, you draw a random normal card. You cannot draw cards with the "draw" tag.
    • Miller: You are seen as a member of the mafia throughout the game, but you are unaware of this, but will reveal as this role upon death, if your role is revealed. You are unable to draw "negative" cards.
    • Cheater: You are seen as holding mafia cards throughout the entire game, you are unaware of this, but will reveal as this role upon death, if your role is revealed. You are unable to draw "negative" cards.
  • Mafia
    • Godfather: Is always seen innocent and a regular town member to alignment investigating cards. Cards that would grant this effect cannot roll for this role.
    • Bouncer: Has permanent protection against any sort of card attacks, any cards that would grant this role protection cannot roll for it.
    • Traitor: Is unable to see the mafia chat at game start, is unaware of who the mafia is and cannot perform the factional kill. The mafia is unaware of who the traitor is. Once per game, the traitor may choose to join the mafia chat, revoking all of the beforementioned, as their night action, but losing all of their currently held cards as the consequence. While seperated, this role is seen as innocent to alignment investigating cards. The Traitor may convert starting ngiht 3.

Phases

Starting Phase: Daystart Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Day Starting Time: At 20 UTC0 -> 20 UTC 0 = 4:00 p.m. in your timezone

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled starting day 2, 24 hours after the day started. - If a player is voted by half of the currently living players +1 then the day immediately ends with that players death. - This will also happen immediately after 24h are passed, should this requirement already be fullfilled. NoLynch: Enabled starting day 2 - If this option has the most votes at the end of the day then nobody dies. If this option is voted by half of the living players +1, then the day immedaitely ends with nobody dying. Ties: Resolved by RNG - If two or more players are tied in votes when the day ends, a random one is chosen to be eliminated. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is eliminated.

Private Chat and Mafia Mechanics

Mafia/Neighbourhoods have a 24/7 private chat. The Mafia factional kill is enforced, if a target is not chosen, a random player will be killed.

Activity Requirements

Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 25 times each 24 hours of a day phase. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 25 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. Replacements: Each slot may only see one replacement total, after which it will instead be modkilled if another replacement becomes necessary. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1 hour or more each day).

Questions

Since this game features a lot of unique mechanics, ask me questions whenever you have any, I will try to help you out. You can find the cards as well as a legend on what each part of the card means on the game-related server linked below.

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them. Breaking our rules can lead to punishments, such as game bans, or forceful replacement by a different player, which I would like to avoid if possible.
