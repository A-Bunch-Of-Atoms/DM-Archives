New Years' Resolutions (Forbidden Actions)

Each player, before they receive their roles, will create a "New Year's Resolution", which will be put into a pool with all the other Resolutions. -# Resolutions may be tweaked if they are too difficult or unfair. Each player, alongside their roles, will receive a Resolution. If a player goes against their Resolution, then they will die, flipping at EOD. Night resolution deaths will flip at following EOD. Player list will be scaled up depending on how many players are in the game.

Phases

Starting Phase: Daystart Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled starting day 2. - If a player is voted by more than half of the currently living players then the day immediately ends with that player’s death. No Lynch: Enabled - If this option has the most votes at the end of the day then nobody dies. If this option is voted by over half the players then the day immediately ends with nobody dying. Ties: Resolved by Priority. - If two or more players are tied in votes when the day ends, the player who first reached the number of votes in their final total without subsequently dipping below it shall be lynched. No Votes: No Lynch - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, nobody will be lynched.

Private Chat and Mafia Mechanics

- The Mafia has a 24/7 private chat on their own private server. - Any other private chats are 24/7 in their own server.

Activity Requirements

Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 25 times each 24 hours of a day phase or you will get prodded. If you get 3 prods, you will be forced replaced. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 25 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. - If you get prodded by making 0 posts in a 24h period, you will receive 2 prods instead. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1 hour or more each day).

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them. Breaking our rules can lead to punishments, such as game bans, or forceful replacement by a different player.

Additional Servers

Please make sure you have enough space on your Discord to join additional servers for this game.
  • Please join the following server, it is required for this game.
    • https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
  • Please ensure you are able to potentially join another additional servers, such as mafia chat or mason chat.
