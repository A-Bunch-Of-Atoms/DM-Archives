A standard game of Diplomacy has 7 players excluding the host. The rules to Diplomacy can be found here. The game will be resolved through backstabbr but actions will be submitted and processed through me. This game may take a while, and is socially heavy please only join if you can handle it

Deadlines

Spring/Fall: 48 hours, Spring 1901 will be 72 hours Summer/Winter: 24 hours, can be extended to 48 hours on weekends if requested.

Other Rulings

  • If 7 game years have passed without a center switching hands, the game will end in a draw with all active powers.
  • When issuing convoy orders, I will accept either implicit orders (A Lon - Pic) or explicit orders (A Lon - Eng - Pic).
  • If you submit invalid orders, I will not tell you unless you explicitly ask.
  • Do not attempt to prove private communications to other players; if you want to tell X what Y said to you, you should say it second-hand.
    • By extension you cannot attempt to prove that you are making particular actions, you must only claim you are doing so
  • There will be a public discussion channel on this server and private discussions will take place in a seperate server (not the playerchat server), which you will be able to do the following.
    • Have 1 on 1 chats with each other power
    • Create combined group chats with multiple powers
    • Have a personal channel for you to make notes and submit actions
  • A phase skip may happen if all players request it, this must be done privately but can be spoken about
  • Failure to submit a round will result in a prod. Two prods in a row will result in you being replaced out.
    • If a replacement is unable to be found in a timely manner, out of respect for the future player and game integrity I will perform the actions for the slot within reason until I mod-kill the slot.
