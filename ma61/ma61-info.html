Need a game without PRs where it’s purely about finding alignment? Well this is the one for you. The game is entirely open and there are no hidden or semi hidden gimmicks/surprises! Enjoy the game in its purest form, chat deduction! Your powers consist of your voice and your votes. Use them well! 15 players 3 scum/wolves/mafia 12 town/citizen/vanilla townie

Mechanics

Days phases: 48 hours long Nights: 24 hours long Majority: Enabled from D1 with a 12 hour cooldown on the first day Plurality: Enabled No-Lynch: Not enabled Evils Chat: 24/7 available Nightkill: Mandatory, with no selection kill is randomised Flips: Alignment/Full Tied Votes: First to Reach No Votes: Random lynch Prod Timers: 25 GAME-RELATED posts every 24 hours. 2 prod warnings, third one means you'll be force replaced. Host can make exceptions to prodding/replacing.
