Welcome to CO15's Anything Goes Upick!

A upick is a game mode where you submit a role name, and I make a role for you themed around that name. Roles can take up any alignment, and the name does not influence what you get, besides the theme of the role.

Phase Mechanics

Days: 48h Nights: 24h Default Daystart: 17 UTC+0

Vote Mechanics

Majority: Enabled Plurality: Enabled Skipping: Enabled Ties: RNG eliminaton Everyone Abstaining: RNG elimination

Private Chats

Private chat servers will be open 24/7 unless stated otherwise if established through an ability.

Feedback

Flips: Full role flips Kill Credit: Disabled Feedback: Rich, in the sense that you learn what happens to you. Default Feedback: "You Slept Peacefully."

Activity Requirements

Minimum: 25 posts / 24h Warnings: 2, third warning = kick The game will be closed. However depending on the final setup, some things may be revealed to you before, during or after the game start.
  • Read #📜rules
  • Only join if you can actually put in the time to play.
  • Join https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
    • Make sure you can join 2-3 more servers if necessary.
  • After the game, a voluntary MVP vote is held, the MVP gains a custom role for a month.
