Time Bubble

Envelop a target into a time bubble each night, if they would die that night, the time bubble activates, protecting them. This has the following effects:

    • It will further be announced, that they are enveloped in a time bubble, and may continue to talk, but cannot be interacted with in any other way.

  • The player may no longer vote or use abilities, but may talk.

Rewind

During the day, choose a player currently affected by Time Bubble to remove the effect from them.
  • They will be removed from the game and flipped normally at the end of the day.

Timetinkerer

  • Unlike anyone else, you may use your Chronoshift ability to revive yourself. You have to do so during night 2 or night 3, not doing so will see you die at the start of day 4.
